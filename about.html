What is OneYou.in ?

Have you ever thought of having your own website to
showcase yourself? This is what exactly oneyou.in
does. It’s an online platform that facilitates you to
have a website of your own.

Why Oneyou.in?

  • You need not worry about purchasing a domain.

  • You need not worry about developing a website.

  • You can customize your presence as per your tastes & preferences.

  • We take care of your portfolio at a minimum cost


    • Finally, the most important “One Link that makes your presence special”.

Who is this for?

Students Wishing to exhibit their traits and willing
to explore the world of opportunities are our main
concerns. But it doesn’t limit others. It also extends
to professionals willing to distinguish themselves in
this world of competition.

When did it happen?

As the proverb goes “Necessity is the mother of
Invention”, Our creator was in search of online
opportunities during the pandemic but didn’t find
an online portfolio, where he could easily showcase
his work. As a result, he began working on the
concept which allows students & professionals to
have their online portfolios.

Where one can use it?

Anyone with an Internet connection can use it
or visit it. Therefore, the prospects are infinite.
Your Oneyou portfolio can be included in your job
search or for meeting new people with similar
interests.
